About Us

At Global Construction and Demolition, Inc., we're not just in the business of building structures; we're in the business of building dreams. With a relentless focus on process, efficiency, and your vision, we take pride in turning your aspirations into reality. Our founder, Renatta ‘RL’ Fleming, a woman entrepreneur in a traditionally male-dominated industry, leads our team with a passion for delivering outstanding construction solutions that redefine the building experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to create spaces that inspire, innovate, and endure. We don't just build structures; we craft environments where life's moments unfold, and memories are made. With each project we undertake, we strive to set new benchmarks in quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

As stewards of change, we also recognize our responsibility to transform and support lower income communities. We believe that everyone deserves access to safe, well-designed spaces that uplift and empower - to call HOME. Through strategic partnerships and community engagement, we work tirelessly to extend our expertise and resources to those who need it most.

Why Choose Us

Efficiency at the Core: Our commitment to process and efficiency ensures that your project is executed seamlessly and on schedule. We know that your time is precious, and we make every effort to deliver results promptly without compromising on quality.

Transparent Collaboration: We value open communication and collaboration. We believe in working hand-in-hand with our clients to understand their needs, preferences, and dreams. Your ideas are our inspiration, and your satisfaction is our ultimate goal.

Green and Sustainable: As pioneers in smart, green, and water-efficient homes, we embrace sustainable practices that reflect our respect for the environment and our dedication to creating spaces that stand the test of time.

Personalized Approach: We know that no two dreams are the same. That's why we approach each project with a personalized touch, tailoring our solutions to your unique vision and requirements.

Problem Solvers: Challenges are opportunities for us to excel. Our proactive approach to problem-solving ensures that we identify and address potential issues before they impact the project, maintaining the integrity of your vision and budget.

Process and Precision: Our meticulous attention to detail and precision in every aspect of construction ensures that your project is executed flawlessly, exceeding your expectations in craftsmanship and aesthetics.

Founder's Vision: Led by Renatta ‘RL’ Fleming, a visionary entrepreneur who understands the power of a smile, we infuse warmth and professionalism into every interaction. With a blend of assured competence and an approachable demeanor, we create an experience that resonates with each client.

About the Owner

R.L. Fleming

R.L. Fleming has had an entrepreneurial spirit since the age of 19, while attending college, she recognized the need for trustworthy management after discovering artists were signing bogus contracts. From that point, she worked hard to build her brand and established several businesses. She finally discovered her passion in the industry of Construction.

She discovered this passion when her grandfather took her to clients homes and renovated, fixed whatever was needed. She became brave enough to embark on establishing her own Construction company in 2022, Global Construction and Demolition. She continues to grow this business and establish a legacy for her 3 children. She takes pride in every project as she listens and learns from her clients to create their dream home, office, or commercial space. Her passion is to make every project unique to every client and exceeds their expectations.
